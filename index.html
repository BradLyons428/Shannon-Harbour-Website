Discover Shannon Harbour Village

Banagher offers peace, history, and natural beauty along the banks of the River Shannon. This town is a hidden gem in the centre of Ireland, ideal for nature lovers, boaters, and anyone seeking a peaceful rural escape.

While small in size, Banagher is rich in charm and heritage. A weekend is perfect to explore its towpaths, enjoy riverside walks, visit historic churches, and soak in the tranquility of life by the water. It's an ideal retreat for those looking to unplug and reconnect with nature.
